About Us

Bubbly scrubber is an initiative taken by Sandeep out of his fondness for automobiles. It's a mobile car wash service and one of a kind, Providing a wide range of car cleaning options. Bubbly scrubber operates on the principle of maximum convenience for the customers by offering services at their doorstep with just a call. Bubbly scrubber caters to different cleaning requirements and budget.
We are very keen on providing the right cleaning solution for automobiles and driving customer satisfaction.
The team is highly passionate about cars and hence is highly skilled in handling cars of various types.. we also understand the emotional connect that many people have with their cars and bikes and we're particular about handling them with utmost care we determine towards taking every possible step for customer satisfaction
